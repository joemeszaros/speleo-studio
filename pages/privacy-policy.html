Privacy Policy for Speleo Studio

Last Updated:

1. Introduction

Speleo Studio ("we", "our", or "the application") is a web-based application for cave visualization and survey management. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our application.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This application is designed to run entirely in your web browser, with minimal data collection and maximum user control.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Local Storage Data

Speleo Studio stores data locally in your web browser using:

  • LocalStorage: Application settings, preferences, and configuration
  • IndexedDB: Cave survey data, projects, and 3D models

All data stored locally remains on your device and is never transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly choose to use the Google Drive integration feature (see section 3).

2.2 Google Drive Integration (Optional)

If you choose to use the Google Drive integration feature:

  • We request access to your Google Drive account through OAuth 2.0 authentication
  • We store OAuth tokens locally in your browser for authentication purposes
  • We sync your cave projects and survey data to folders in your Google Drive account
  • We do NOT access any other files or data in your Google Drive account

The Google Drive integration is completely optional. You can use Speleo Studio fully without enabling this feature.

2.3 No Personal Information Collection

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, address, etc.)
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Device information or browser fingerprints
  • Location data
  • Any data that you do not explicitly provide or create within the application

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Local Data

Data stored locally in your browser is used solely to:

  • Provide the core functionality of the application
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Enable offline functionality

3.2 Google Drive Data

If you enable Google Drive integration, your data is used to:

  • Create backup copies of your cave projects and survey data
  • Enable synchronization across multiple devices (if you access the same Google account)
  • Prevent data loss

Your data stored in Google Drive is subject to Google's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT share, sell, or disclose your data to any third parties, except:

  • Google Drive: Only if you explicitly enable the Google Drive integration feature. In this case, your data is stored in your personal Google Drive account, which is governed by Google's privacy policies.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

5. Data Security

Since Speleo Studio runs entirely in your web browser:

  • All data processing occurs locally on your device
  • No data is transmitted to external servers (except when using Google Drive integration)
  • OAuth tokens for Google Drive are stored securely in your browser's local storage
  • You have full control over your data and can delete it at any time by clearing your browser's storage

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: All your data is stored locally in your browser. You can access it through your browser's developer tools.
  • Deletion: You can delete all application data by clearing your browser's LocalStorage and IndexedDB for this website.
  • Export: You can export your cave projects and survey data in various formats (JSON, CSV, DXF, PNG) at any time.
  • Google Drive Disconnection: You can disconnect the Google Drive integration at any time through the application settings.

7. Third-Party Services

7.1 Google Drive API

If you use the Google Drive integration, your use of Google Drive is subject to:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Terms of Service

We only request the minimum necessary permissions to sync your cave data to Google Drive.

7.2 Open Source Libraries

Speleo Studio uses the following open-source libraries that do not collect user data:

  • Three.js - 3D graphics rendering
  • Tabulator.js - Data table management

8. Children's Privacy

Speleo Studio is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete such information.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
